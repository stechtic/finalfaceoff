Quick Brief Of 2023


The year 2023 was one of the remarkable year in the history of Cambrian League(Though it was our First year).This Year we have seen an incredible play by our players.Ranging from Abhinav Best Saves to Pratyush's great headers.We have seen Prashant's Incredible lightning speed to Adhyan's cleanliest tackles'. The Best Assists By Akshar,Shivam ,Anjal turned the tables round. Not only this the great midfielders of the cambrian league including Abhay,Shorya,Abhishek and Akshat which when dribble threatened the opponent's defenders. And the last but not the least the defenders and goalkeepers ,which in my opinion are most important part of the team.Aryan,Krishna,Shiven and Augustya were the defenders which played an important role in their team victory.Adhyan ,whose speed and tackles made the strikers and the midfielder nervous.Daksh whose defending doesn't let the strikers to even shoot towards the goal.

At last I(though i didn't discussed about myself)would like to say that I really enjoyed the three seasons of Cambrian League.
